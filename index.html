Our Story

Authentic Texas BBQ with a Bite

Low & Slow, That's How We Roll

At Black Widow BBQ, we're passionate about authentic Texas-style barbecue. Based in Salado, Texas, we bring the rich traditions of Central Texas smoked meats to your table with our mobile catering services.

Every cut is hand-selected, seasoned with our signature rub, and smoked low and slow over premium hardwoods until it reaches perfection. From tender, juicy brisket to succulent pulled pork, we put our heart into every bite.

Whether it's a backyard celebration, corporate event, or wedding reception, Black Widow BBQ delivers an unforgettable dining experience that your guests will be talking about for years to come.
